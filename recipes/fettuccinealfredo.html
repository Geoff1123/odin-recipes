Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, a layered masterpiece of rich flavors and hearty textures. This classic Italian dish features sheets of tender pasta nestled between layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted cheese. The slow-simmered sauce, infused with garlic, onions, and herbs, creates a deep, robust flavor that melds beautifully with the cheese and pasta. Baked to golden perfection, each bite delivers a perfect balance of creamy, tangy, and savory goodness.

Whether served as a cozy family dinner or an impressive dish for guests, lasagna is a timeless favorite. The bubbling cheese on top forms a deliciously crisp crust, giving way to a luscious, saucy interior. Customizable with different fillings like spinach, mushrooms, or spicy sausage, it's a versatile dish that can be tailored to any taste. Served hot with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad, lasagna is a satisfying and indulgent meal that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Fettucine Pasta
  • 2 Sticks of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • Salt to tatse
  • Pepper to taste
  • Garlic Powder to taste
  • 1 1/4 cup of Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Mise en place all Ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
