Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is the epitome of creamy, indulgent pasta dishes, featuring tender ribbons of fettuccine coated in a luxuriously rich Parmesan cheese sauce. Made with butter, heavy cream, and freshly grated cheese, the sauce clings to each strand, creating a velvety texture with every bite. The simplicity of its ingredients allows the deep, nutty flavor of Parmesan to shine, while the buttery richness makes it a dish that feels both comforting and decadent.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Fettucine Pasta
  • 2 Sticks of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • Salt to tatse
  • Pepper to taste
  • Garlic Powder to taste
  • 1 1/4 cup of Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Mise en place all Ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
